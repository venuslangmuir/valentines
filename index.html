VALENTINE’S DAY RECIPES

Pomegranate Cocktail

Keep your chocolates and candy hearts. If you want to prove how much you love someone, you’ll mix up this pomegranate hummus.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/8 cup Bai Ipanema Pomegranate
  • ¼ cup Tahini
  • 1/8 cup Lemon juice (about one lemon)
  • ½ clove Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 1 14oz can of Chickpeas
  • 1 tsp of Paprika
  • Pomegranate seeds

DIRECTIONS:

Rinse and drain the chickpeas.  Add tahini, lemon juice and Ipanema Pomegranate to the bowl of your food processor. Pulse for about 1 minute, scraping the edges with a spatula as needed. Add garlic, olive oil, and salt. Pulse again until smooth. Add chickpeas. Pulse until you reach the desired consistency, adding a little bit more of Ipanema Pomegranate as needed. Top with pomegranate seeds.

Must be 21+. Please Drink Responsibly.
